Lil Skies - Roses

"Roses" is a song by American rapper Lil Skies, released on January 10, 2018. It is the second single from his debut studio album "Life of a Dark Rose". The song was produced by Taz Taylor, Nick Mira, and E-Trou.

The lyrics of "Roses" focus on Lil Skies' success and the sacrifices he has made to get where he is. He reflects on his journey to fame, and how he's overcome struggles and criticism from others. The chorus features a catchy melody and the phrase "I'm sorry, but I cannot change", which has become a popular catchphrase among Lil Skies' fans.

"Roses" was a commercial success for Lil Skies, peaking at number 69 on the US Billboard Hot 100 and reaching Platinum certification by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) in July 2018.

The music video for "Roses" was directed by Cole Bennett of Lyrical Lemonade and features Lil Skies performing the song while surrounded by flowers and roses. The video has gained over 300 million views on YouTube as of April 2023.

Overall, "Roses" has been considered one of Lil Skies' most popular and successful songs, and has helped to establish him as a rising star in the rap scene.
