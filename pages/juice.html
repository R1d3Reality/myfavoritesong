Juice WRLD - Lucid dreams

"Lucid Dreams" is a song by American rapper and singer Juice WRLD, released on May 4, 2018, as the lead single from his debut studio album "Goodbye & Good Riddance". The song was produced by Nick Mira and debuted at number 74 on the US Billboard Hot 100, eventually peaking at number 2.

The lyrics of "Lucid Dreams" focus on Juice WRLD's heartbreak over a failed relationship and his struggle to move on from his ex. The song samples the melody from Sting's 1993 hit "Shape of My Heart", which adds a melancholic tone to the track. Juice WRLD's emotional delivery and vulnerable lyrics resonated with listeners, especially younger fans who related to the themes of love and loss.

The music video for "Lucid Dreams" was directed by Cole Bennett of Lyrical Lemonade and features Juice WRLD wandering through a dark, dreamlike world, reflecting on his past relationship. The video has gained over 800 million views on YouTube as of April 2023.

"Lucid Dreams" has been a commercial and critical success for Juice WRLD, receiving widespread acclaim for its honest and vulnerable lyrics. The song has been certified 7x Platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) and has helped to establish Juice WRLD as one of the most promising new talents in the rap and hip-hop scene.

Unfortunately, Juice WRLD passed away on December 8, 2019, at the age of 21 due to a drug overdose. "Lucid Dreams" has since become a tribute to his legacy, with many fans and fellow artists acknowledging the impact he had on the music industry and the influence he had on young listeners.
