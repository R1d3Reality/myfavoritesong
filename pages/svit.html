The chorus features the repeated phrase "Такі ми були молоді" (translates to "That's how we were when we were young"), which has become a popular catchphrase among the band's fans.

The music video for "Молодість" was directed by Dmytro Viter and features footage of the band performing the song in a dark, moody setting. The video has gained over 1 million views on YouTube as of April 2023.

"Молодість" has been a critical and commercial success for Sad Svit, helping to establish the band as one of the most promising new acts in the Ukrainian music scene. The song's nostalgic and introspective lyrics, combined with its catchy chorus and upbeat instrumentals, have resonated with listeners of all ages.
